About

Scrim Finder is an application allowing competitive gamers a place to find and organise practice matches with other teams, and to spectate and learn from other matches.

Players are able to browse all the games, teams and current matches. Once a player has signed up for Scrim Finder, they have the ability to either browse and join an existing team, or to create their own. Players are then able to create a match with another team at a particular time they are available, and then other teams are able to view these times and book a match with the team that posted it.

This application gives gamers an easy way to find and play with each other, making it much easier to get in practice or just for fun.
